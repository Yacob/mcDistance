The McDistance

The McDistance is a dynamic measurement unit. It measures the distance between you and the nearest McDonalds. You are always 1 McDistance from the nearest McDonalds or you are at McDonalds.

What if you're at McDonalds?

If you are at McDonalds you have a McDistance of 0 which implies that you shouldn't be measuring how far you are from anything because you shouldn't be leaving the great McDonalds

What if you are exactly between two McDonalds?

Then you got options. Both are one McDistance from you

Is the McDistance measured as the bird flys or by path?

Actually the McDistance is measured by the path Google Maps chooses. Apple Maps can be used as a poor man's substitute to to estimate McDistances.
